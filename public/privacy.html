Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Draft notice: This policy is a starting template based on how the product actually handles data as of the date above. It is not legal advice. Before relying on it for a live, paying customer base, have it reviewed by a lawyer familiar with privacy law in the jurisdictions you operate in (including GDPR/UK GDPR and Australian Privacy Principles if applicable to your customers).

This policy explains what information Local Visibility Audit ("we," "us") collects when you use our website and application (the "Service"), how we use it, and who we share it with.

Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address and password (stored as a salted hash — we never store or can retrieve your plain-text password)
  • Company/agency name, if provided
  • Branding assets you upload (logo image, "prepared by" name)

Report data

  • Business names and locations you enter to generate reports
  • Publicly available business and competitor data retrieved from Google Business Profile via our data provider (Outscraper) at the time each report runs
  • The generated report content, stored so you can access your report history

Billing information

Payment card details are collected and processed directly by our payment processor, Square — we never see or store your full card number. We retain your subscription tier, billing cycle, and billing status to manage your account.

Usage and technical data

  • Session cookies used solely to keep you logged in (no advertising or cross-site tracking cookies)
  • Standard server logs (IP address, browser type, timestamps) for security and troubleshooting

How we use this information

  • To provide and operate the Service, including generating and storing your reports
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional email (e.g. reports you choose to send, account notifications)
  • To provide customer support
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the Service

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your data to train third-party advertising products.

Third parties we share data with

We use the following service providers to operate the Service. Each processes only the data necessary for their function:

  • Outscraper — retrieves the public Google Business Profile and competitor data used in your reports
  • OpenRouter and its underlying AI model providers — process report data to generate the written report content
  • Square — processes subscription payments
  • Resend — delivers transactional email, including reports you choose to send to clients
  • Railway — hosts our application and database infrastructure

Data retention

We retain your account and report history for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data and stored reports within a reasonable period, except where we're required to retain billing records for legal or tax purposes.

Your rights

You can access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing hello@localvisibilityaudit.com. Depending on where you're located, you may have additional rights under applicable law (such as the right to data portability or to object to certain processing).

Children's privacy

The Service is intended for business use and is not directed at, or knowingly used by, children under 16.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to hello@localvisibilityaudit.com.
