Refund Policy

Last updated: 21 July 2026

Draft notice: This is a starting template, not legal advice. Refund and cooling-off requirements vary by jurisdiction and payment processor — have this reviewed against the consumer law that applies to your customers before publishing live.

Free trial

Every plan includes a 14-day free trial, and no payment method is required to start one. Use the trial to generate real reports and evaluate the Service before you're ever charged.

Subscription charges

Because each report draws on real, metered third-party data the moment it's generated, and because you have a full trial period to evaluate the Service first, subscription charges are generally non-refundable once a billing period has started. This applies to both monthly and annual plans.

Cancelling your subscription

You can cancel anytime from your account. Cancellation stops future renewals — you keep access through the end of the period you've already paid for, and you will not be charged again. We don't offer partial refunds for unused time within a billing period.

Billing errors

If you're charged in error — a duplicate charge, an incorrect amount, or a charge after you cancelled — contact us within 30 days of the charge and we'll issue a full refund for the error once verified.

Report bundles

Overage report bundles are a one-time purchase for reports beyond your plan's monthly allowance. Once a bundle's reports have started being used, the bundle is non-refundable. Unused bundle reports carry over for one additional billing cycle, then expire.

How to request something covered here

Email hello@localvisibilityaudit.com with your account email and a description of the issue. We aim to respond within 24–48 hours.
