📊 Competitor Benchmark Data
Export your top 20 competitors from Outscraper. This becomes the industry benchmark for your audit.
How to get your competitor data:
  1. Go to outscraper.com and use the Google Maps Scraper
  2. Search for your industry in your city — e.g. plumber in Melbourne
  3. Set results to top 20 and export as CSV
  4. Open the CSV, select all, copy and paste it below

Required columns: name, rating, reviews, photos_count, type. Optional but useful: subtypes, reviews_per_score_1, reviews_per_score_5, business_status
