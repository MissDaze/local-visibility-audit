About Us

Why we built this

Local Visibility Audit exists because of a pattern that keeps repeating: a business dominates local search, has hundreds of reviews and a strong rating — and sits on a website that would score close to zero if anyone actually measured it. Or the reverse: a genuinely good business with a weak profile, getting outranked by a competitor who simply shows up more consistently online. Either way, the people trying to point this out — web designers, SEO consultants, marketing agencies, reputation managers — usually have to make the case with opinion instead of evidence.

We built this tool to close that gap. Instead of a vague "visibility score" with no context, every report benchmarks a business against real, live competitors pulled directly from Google Business Profile data at the moment the report runs. No invented statistics, no filled-in templates dressed up as analysis — just a consultant-grade, evidence-based report that happens to be generated in minutes instead of hours.

Who it's for

We're building this specifically for the people who sell visibility, credibility, or growth to local businesses for a living — web designers, freelance marketers, SEO and Google Business Profile consultants, reputation management specialists, and the agencies who run all of the above across a whole client roster. If that's you, the report is meant to slot directly into how you already pitch and report to clients, not become a new tool you have to learn.

Where we are today

We're early. This product is being built and refined in the open, with a direct line between what our first customers need and what gets shipped next. If something about the tool doesn't fit how you work, we want to hear about it — reach us any time at hello@localvisibilityaudit.com.
